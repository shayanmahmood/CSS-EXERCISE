From Garden to Plate

A potager is a French term for an ornamental vegetable or kitchen garden.Often flowers(edible and non-edible) and herbs are planted with vegetables to enhance the garden's beauty. The goal is to make the function of providing food aesthetically pleasing.

What to Plant

Plants are chosen as much for their functionality as for their color and form.Many are trained to grow upward. A well designer potager can provide food,cut flowers and herbs for the home with very little maintenance. Potagers can disguise their function of providing for a home a wide arry of froms-from the carefree style the cottage garden to the fromality of a knot garden.
